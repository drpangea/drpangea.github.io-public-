Top Priorities

Rebuild & Strengthen Middle Class

Building an economy that works for more North Carolinians. Focused on strengthening local supply chains, supporting small business growth, and competitive wages.

“North Carolinians are not looking for handouts; We want to provide for ourselves and families without being in debt our whole lives.” — Loren

Enshrine Healthcare as a constitutional right

Our current healthcare system is both inadequate and inefficient. Holding healthcare or medical treatment for ransom is killing hard‑working Americans for pennies on the dollar.

“Healthcare isn’t a business; it’s a service that every citizen needs in their pursuit of happiness.” — Loren

Artificial Intelligence & Large‑Language‑Model regulations

Artificial Intelligence is here to stay. We must protect jobs, ensure accountability, and direct AI to augment human work rather than replace it.

“Technology should not replace jobs but enhance them. Allowing us to complete our work more efficiently, accurately, and spend more time with those we love.” — Loren
