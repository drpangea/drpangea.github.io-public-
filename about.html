About Loren Bibler

Loren Bibler is a lifelong North Carolinian, community leader, and small business advocate who believes in pragmatic, people‑centered solutions.

Biography

Loren grew up in Sampletown and worked in local small businesses and civic organizations. Loren’s priorities include strengthening the local economy, improving healthcare access, and investing in education and workforce development.
